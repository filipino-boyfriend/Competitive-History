Overview

Filipino Boyfriend is a Filipino-American Super Smash Bros. Ultimate player based in San Diego, California. He has competed in grassroots and collegiate events and currently represents Aztec Esports at San Diego State University.

Super Smash Flash 2

Filipino Boyfriend began competing in community crew battles in 2019 before transitioning to online tournament play in 2020 under the tag PSIPSY, primarily using Pac-Man.

His most notable result was a first-place finish at ARSC Smash Strike 2020. He also qualified for the Oasis Invitational Bracket and competed against several of the game's top players.

Flash Party

He was active in the North American Flash Party community during 2022–2023, founding the Peacekeepers crew and contributing to early grassroots competitive activity.

He achieved multiple top eight placements in online community events.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

From 2019 to 2023, he competed primarily in the Scripps Ranch Power League, a high school tournament circuit that he helped co-organize. During this period, he won multiple events and finished ranked first overall.

Following a competitive hiatus after graduating in 2023, he returned to the San Diego Ultimate scene in late 2024 while attending San Diego State University and later joined the university's Aztec Esports team.

Since returning to competition, he has competed primarily at Aztec Games events and other Southern California tournaments, including a ninth-place finish at Aztec Spring Games 2026.

Notable Results

  • 1st – ARSC Smash Strike 2020 (SSF2)
  • 1st – Scripps Ranch Power League Rankings
  • 4th – Aztec Spring Games 2025 (SSBU)
  • 9th – Aztec Spring Games 2026 (SSBU)

Community Involvement

Outside of competition, Filipino Boyfriend has provided commentary for SSF2, Flash Party, and Aztec Games events in Southern California.
