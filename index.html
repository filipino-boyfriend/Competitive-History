Filipino Boyfriend

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate player from San Diego, California.

Formerly known as PSIPSY and Honor, Filipino Boyfriend is a competitive Super Smash Flash 2 and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate player known for using Pac-Man, Ness, and Pit. He was also briefly a competitive Flash Party player known for using Sivi and Magician.

Overview

Filipino Boyfriend is a Filipino-American Super Smash Bros. Ultimate player based in San Diego, California. He has competed in grassroots and collegiate-level events, including Aztec Esports at San Diego State University.

Main Characters

  • Pac-Man (main)
  • Ness (secondary / former main)
  • Pit (pocket)
  • Ice Climbers (co-main in the making)

Super Smash Flash 2

Filipino Boyfriend began competing in community crew battles in 2019 before transitioning to online bracket play in 2020 under the tag PSIPSY, primarily using Pac-Man.

His most notable result was 1st place at ARSC Smash Strike 2020. He also qualified for the Oasis Invitational Bracket and competed against top North American SSF2 players.

Flash Party

He was active in the North American Flash Party scene during 2022–2023, founding the Peacekeepers crew and contributing to early competitive development in the community.

He achieved multiple top 8 placements in online community tournaments.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

From 2019–2023, he competed in the Scripps Ranch Power League, a high school tournament circuit he helped co-organize, finishing ranked 1st overall.

After a competitive hiatus following graduation in 2023, he returned to the San Diego Ultimate scene in late 2024 while attending San Diego State University, later joining Aztec Esports.

Since returning, he has competed at Aztec Games events and other Southern California tournaments, including a 9th place finish at Aztec Spring Games 2026.

Notable Results

  • 1st – ARSC Smash Strike 2020 (SSF2)
  • 1st – Scripps Ranch Power League Rankings
  • 4th – Aztec Spring Games 2025 (SSBU)
  • 9th – Aztec Spring Games 2026 (SSBU)

Community Involvement

Outside of competition, Filipino Boyfriend has provided commentary for SSF2, Flash Party, and Aztec Games events in Southern California.
